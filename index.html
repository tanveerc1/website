Heaton Park

Boasting of numerous activities that families can engage in, it is the largest park in Manchester and the largest municipal park across Europe. For car access, the SatNav postcode is M25 2SW. MetroLink Trams and Buses are available to get to Heaton Park. Located in Manchester, Heaton Park is possibly the best place to visit for that economical family day out in the North West. It provides ample space, stated greenery and two play areas especially meant for kids besides a host of events, activities, family outings and games along with sports.
